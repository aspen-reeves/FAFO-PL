  • But Why?
    • The WTFPL is a great license, but comes with a few problems:
      • It has no warranty clause, which could lead to legal issues in some countries.
      • Some poeple at least want attribution, and the WTFPL doesn't require it.
    • Some people are fine with these, so I provided the 0-Clause, equivalent to the WTFPL.
      • The 1-Clause is the 0-Clause with a warranty clause.
      • The 2-Clause is the 1-Clause with an attribution clause.
